Instructions to play the Quiz

  1. There are ten questions to answer with three options to choose from.
  2. After you answer the question click the 'Submit' button to check whether you are right or wrong.
  3. Click on the 'Next' button to move to the next question.
  4. Click on to the 'Home' button to skip the quiz.
  5. At the end of the ten questions click on 'Finish' button see your score.
  6. Click on 'Play Again' button to try the quiz again.
